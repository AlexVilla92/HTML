"The force is strong in my family"

This quote was taken from Return Of The Jedi (1983). In it, Luke meets Leia on the planet of Endor before departing on his mission to confront Darth Vader for the final time.
Through these quotes he reveals to her that she is his sister. It was used in the second trailer for The Force Awakens but does not seem to appear in the final film.

Also, there's a minor correction. The first line is, "The Force is strong in my family. My father has it".
